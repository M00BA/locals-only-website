About Locals Only

Locals Only began with a simple wish: to feel closer to people again.

Hamburg and Lübeck are full of life, but it’s still easy to feel alone in the middle of everything. I wanted a place where meeting people felt natural, warm, and human — not forced, not awkward, not digital noise. So I built this space for anyone who wants the same thing.

This project is for people who want small, cozy meetups, gentle conversations, a friendly community, and a place to feel welcome. No pressure. No expectations. Just people meeting people in two beautiful cities.

If you’re looking for community, you’re not the only one. I’m looking for it too.
