About Locals Only

Locals Only began with a simple wish: to feel closer to people again. Hamburg and Lübeck are full of life, but it is still easy to feel alone.

This project is for people who want small, cozy meetups, gentle conversations, and a place to feel welcome. A calm space to meet others in a soft and friendly way.
