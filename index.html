About Locals Only

Locals Only is a small warm space for people in Hamburg and Lübeck who want to meet new friends without pressure. No big crowds and no awkward networking just calm introvert friendly meetups where everyone can relax.

We keep it simple show up be kind and enjoy the moment. Whether you are new in town or just want gentle social energy you are in the right place.
